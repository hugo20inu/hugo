Hello, here we will explain the working of the ecosystem in detail.

4% Token distribution is made to the holders from each purchase, sale, transfer transaction at the rate they have.
1% For every purchase, sale, transfer transaction, 1% of the mentioned tokens are permanently deleted from the supply.
5% Marketing income is obtained from each purchase, sale, transfer; 1% is used for buyback. When you appear in an account that does not receive a reflection profit and does not sell the token, purchases are made to add permanent value to the project. By adding 2% liquidity, a stronger token liquidity is tried to be obtained. 2% is used to cover project expenses.

Some accounts within the ecosystem have taken their place in the ecosystem with the reflection income in order to generate income and advertise and airdrop to the Project. These accounts will be specified in detail with an update in the future.

Combustion and pinksale lockout wallets are also excluded from reflection revenue. they cannot earn any income from these accounts.
